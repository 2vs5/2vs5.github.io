Daeho Lee

I work at the intersection of bioinformatics and machine learning, focusing on single-cell and spatial transcriptomics. My current research develops scalable methods for integrating scRNA-seq with image-based and spatial data using anchor-based optimal transport, and benchmarks preprocessing steps such as ambient RNA removal to improve downstream analyses. I also have experience in biomedical text mining and drug-related knowledge extraction.
